Mailto — Contact Form Demo

Fill in the form below and click Send Message to see exactly what happens when a visitor contacts you via the mailto: approach.

⚡ How It Works

1
Visitor fills the form
They enter their name, email, subject and message on your portfolio page.
↓
2
Click "Send Message"
The form data is collected and converted into a mailto: link with pre-filled content.
↓
3
Email app opens automatically
The visitor's default email client (Gmail, Outlook, Apple Mail) opens with everything pre-filled — To, Subject, and Message body.
↓
4
Visitor clicks Send in their email app
The email is sent from their own email address directly to your inbox. You can reply directly to them.

Pros

  • Zero setup, no account
  • Works completely offline
  • Free, unlimited messages
  • Reply goes directly to sender
  • No API keys needed

Cons

  • Visitor needs email app installed
  • Desktop may not have default app set
  • Visitor must click Send themselves
  • No confirmation on portfolio page

Key difference from EmailJS: With mailto:, the email is sent from the visitor's own email account — not from a server. This means you can reply directly to them. However, visitors on a desktop PC without a configured email client (like Outlook) may see nothing happen when they click Send. On all mobile phones and Macs, it always works perfectly.
